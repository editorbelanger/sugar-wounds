About the Band

From the creator of Tanpopo Crisis and Fucked, Sugar Wounds is a solo grindgaze project. Each release brings dissonance and atmosphere together, with albums including Calico Dreams, Mechanical Friends, and most recently Change of Heart, a split with Sachiel.
